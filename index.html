01About

Ten years of helping
organizations communicate
through story.

My background is in documentary filmmaking and creative leadership, but the part of the work I've always been most drawn to is the human side of communication — figuring out how to take something complex, technical, or abstract and make it feel relatable and meaningful to real people.

In my current role at 12 Stars Media, I lead storytelling and communications projects for organizations across healthcare, technology, energy, manufacturing, and nonprofit spaces. A lot of that work focuses on employee engagement, recruitment, organizational culture, executive messaging, and mission-driven communications.

Over the years I've worked with global teams, managed productions across more than 20 countries, and partnered closely with executives, employees, scientists, engineers, patients, and community leaders. I'm especially interested in the role storytelling can play inside organizations — helping people feel connected to a mission, creating clarity around complex work, and building trust through honest, human communication.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about fly fishing, photography, and going on adventures with my kids.
